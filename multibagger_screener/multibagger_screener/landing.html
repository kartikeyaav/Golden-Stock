PIPELINE

Four moves, every single night

No screens to watch, no scans to remember. The system runs after close, in the cloud, and reports in.

▸

Scan the universe

Fresh EOD data for 651 small, mid & microcaps. Every chart tagged by stage — basing, confirmed uptrend, extended, or broken.

▸

Score & alert

Alerts fire only on state transitions — a base completing, a breakout triggering. Each carries an 8-dimension conviction score and hard governance vetoes.

▸

AI deep-dives

An AI analyst researches the top candidates on the open web the same night; a weekly AI committee picks and writes full investment theses.

▸

Plan & track

Every buy signal ships with a sized two-lot plan — entry, stop, partial, trail. Every call lands in a forward journal that grades the system itself.

CAPABILITIES

A full research desk, condensed to software

Stage & breakout engine

Weinstein stage tags, Minervini trend template, VCP base detection and volume-confirmed pivot breakouts — the entry edge, mechanically applied to every chart.

ENTRIES ARE 100% TECHNICAL

Nightly autonomous ops

Runs in the cloud after every market close — scan, score, research, publish, notify. Zero dependence on any laptop being awake.

365 NIGHTS · HANDS-FREE

Conviction with vetoes

Eight scored dimensions — momentum, earnings inflection, theme, smart money, strength, catalyst, governance, valuation. Pledged promoters and governance red flags cap the score, no matter how pretty the chart.

COVERAGE-HONEST SCORING

AI research layers

A nightly analyst deep-dives top alerts; a weekly committee curates 3–5 researched picks with theses, catalysts and risks. AI advises — it can never widen a stop or override a veto.

MACHINE QUALIFIES · AI EXPLAINS

News radar

Every NSE filing since the last scan, whitelist-classified — order wins, M&A, approvals, probes, pledges — and cross-referenced with tonight's chart state. Positive news on an actionable chart = research first.

NEWS MOVES ATTENTION, NEVER ENTRIES

Self-auditing journal

Every signal, verdict and paper trade is logged forward and graded against its own plan. The system must beat its own baseline with out-of-sample evidence — or the feature is cut.
